PACMAN GAME

Pac-Man is a classic arcade game released in 1980 by Namco. The game was designed by Toru Iwatani and features the iconic character of Pac-Man, a yellow, circular character who eats pellets while avoiding four ghosts. The game became a cultural phenomenon and helped to establish video games as a mainstream form of entertainment.

Pac-Man's popularity was due to its addictive gameplay and colorful graphics, which kept players coming back for more. The game's iconic characters, such as Pac-Man and the four ghosts, have become instantly recognizable and have spawned numerous sequels, merchandise, and even a cartoon show. Today, Pac-Man remains a beloved classic and has been ported to numerous platforms, including modern consoles and mobile devices. Its enduring popularity is a testament to its timeless gameplay and iconic characters, and it continues to be enjoyed by fans around the world.
